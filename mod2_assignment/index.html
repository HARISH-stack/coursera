TYPES OF TRIANGLES

EQUILATERAL TRAINGLE

*An equilateral triangle has all sides the same length.
*It is also a regular polygon with all angles measuring 60Â°.

ISOCELES TRIANGLE

*An isosceles triangle has two sides of equal length.
*An isosceles triangle also has two angles of the same measure.

SCALENE TRIANGLE

*A scalene triangle has all its sides of different lengths.
*Equivalently, it has all angles of different measure.
